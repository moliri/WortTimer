About Wort Timer

Wort Timer is a mobile web app for home brewers that allows one to time out the different steps of the brewing process.

Creators: Mark Padecky and Milim Lee

Developers: Dylan Hirshkowitz, Leesha Maliakal, Zachariah Straight, Haodong Wang, Peiying Zhou, and Daniel Zuo
